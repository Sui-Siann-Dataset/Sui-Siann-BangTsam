Sui Siann Dataset
Taioanoe speech dataset

Tâi-bûn pán

Tse sī tsi̍t pau siann-im ê gí-liāu-khòo. Tī 2018 nî iau-tshiánn Ông Siù-iông(王秀容) lāu-su lâi lo̍k-im. Lóng-tsóng ū 2,897 tè im-tóng, ta̍k tè tn̂g-té ū OO kàu OO, liōng-kî-iok tsóng 7 tiám-tsing.

Gí-kíng tsīn-liōng tsiâu-tsn̂g, tuì suànn-bûn kàu ji̍t-siông tuì-uē. Suànn-bûn sī 2015 nî tshut-pán--ê. Ta̍k kù lóng ū hàn-jī kah Lô-má-jī.

Im-tóng ê siū-khuân sī CC BY-SA. M̄-koh thiann-phah-kó kan-na ē-táng iōng tī gí-im ha̍p-sîng, ha̍k-su̍t--ê a̍h-sī sing-lí--ê lóng ē-īng-tit. Put-tsún siu-kái thiann-phah-kó ê hàn-jī. Nā-tsún lí m̄ sī beh iōng tī gí-im ha̍p-sîng, tshiánn liân-lo̍k tsok-tsiá.

Pún kè-uē ū tshíng-tio̍h Bûn-huà-pōo 2017 nî póo-tsōo.

English ver.

This is a speech dataset consisting of 2,897 short audio clips of a single speaker Ms.Ông Siù-iông reading articles. A transcription is provided for each clip. Clips vary in length from "??? to ???" seconds and have a total length of approximately 7 hours.

The dataset are designed with situation balance, from articles to conversation. The articles were published in 2015. Each sentence pair contains Chinese line and Lomaji line.

The audio is CC BY-SA license. The transcription is only allowed in speech synthesis project, academically or commercially, and the remix, rewrite of Chinese lines are not allowed. If there is any other usage purposes, please contact the author.

This project received financial aid by Taiwan Ministry of Culture in 2017.

File Format

Tshing-tuann hō sui-siann.csv. Tóng-àn-lāi tsi̍t pit tuì tsi̍t tè im-tóng, iōng tāu-hō (,) keh--khui. Lāi-bīn:

  1. 音檔: Im-tong miâ (.wav)
  2. 來源: Tuì tá tsi̍t phinn bûn-tsiunn (UTF-8)
  3. 漢字: Guân-kó. Sòo-jī tsiàu liām--ê kái--kuè, tshiūnn 100 kái-tsò 一百 (UTF-8)
  4. 羅馬字: Tâi-lô, siông-sè tī phiau-kì guân-tsik(UTF-8)

Lo̍k-im keh-sik ū nn̄g khuán.

  1. Tâi-tāi: 44100 Hz, 16-bit
  2. Muscene Studio: 48000 Hz, 24-bit
